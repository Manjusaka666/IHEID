This folder contains the Stata replication files to generate the figures, tables and regressions results of the paper 
"Sovereign Debt Relief and its Aftermath" by Carmen M. Reinhart and Christoph Trebesch

The analysis was conducted with Stata version 12.1

- The do-file "EMEs_do.do" replicates the results for the 1978-2010 sample of emerging market economies (including the Baker and Brady debt relief initiatives). The corresponding data file is called "EMEs.dta".

- The do-file "Interwar_do.do" replicates the results for interwar years (including the Hoover Moratorium and the 1934 debt relief event). The corresponding data file is called "Interwar.dta".
